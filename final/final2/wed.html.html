OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide as much help we can for those going through hard times and struggling to seek help. We believe that everyone that seeks help should be helped.

HARMONY

Na Haumana is a hawaiian word that means the scholars. Na Haumana is a group made up of three girls who believe that there should be harmony among all of us. Both love and respect are needed to have a stable relationship with one another. Love is a feeling that can be manipulated in many cases like in toxic relationships where the victim can be brainwashed into believing they are not good enough. We don not believe that people should be treated like this. We believe people should live in harmony although at times there might be arguements it should never come to abuse.
